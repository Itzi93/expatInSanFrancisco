Where to do the grocery shopping
in San Francisco?

San Francisco is a city with an extensive public transport network. Below we show you what are the public transport methods you will find in San Francisco.

Safeway

Safeway is one of the most famous supermarkets in San Francisco. In it you can find a variety of products and brands with good value for money.
It is important that you take out the customer card before buying, since with them you will receive many discounts when you have to pay.

Trader Joe's

Trader Joe's is a neighborhood grocery store with amazing food and drink from around the globe and around the corner. Great quality at great prices. All their products are fresh and with their own brand.

7-Eleven

7-Eleven is your go-to convenience store for food, snacks, hot and cold beverages, gas and so much more. Generally open 24 hours a day.

Wallgreens & CVS Pharmacy

Both have pharmacy service, health & wellness products, health information and photo services. in some they also sell basic food products and household products.
As in Safeway, it is ideal to register as a client so that the available discounts apply.

Whole Foods

It's an American supermarket chain exclusively featuring foods without artificial preservatives, colors, flavors, sweeteners, etc. It's more expensive that the other supermarkets but the quality is exceptional.
