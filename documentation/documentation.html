There are three types of visa to enter the United States: tourist, work and student.

Work visa

To enter in the country with any type of work visa, you must present at the immigration border your passport with the visa on it and the document i-129 of which you must take 2 copies stamped by the Embassy of the United States.

Tourist visa

For this type of visa you must present your passport along with the ESTA that will allow you to stay for a maximum stay of 90 days.

Social Security Number

It is important that you get the social security number if you go to work there as soon as possible, the day after arriving you can go to some of the offices that are in the city and you will not receive it for a maximum of 2 weeks.

Driver License

If you are going to stay longer than 3 months, it is advisable that you get a driver's license at the San Francisco DMV.
In order to obtain your driver's license or California ID, you must present a copy of I-94, a passport with a visa and two documents certifying that you are living in the city.
It consists of a theoretical test that you can do in Spanish / English and a driving test.

Medical insurance

In the USA, the medical system is very different from Spain, so if you are going to live in the USA we recommend that you already have a private medical insurance contract, although if you are going to work for a season contracted by the same company, it is possible that they themselves they manage a private medical insurance that covers all the needs you may have.

Expat with family

If you are thinking of moving with your partner / family. In the first case you must be married so that your partner is accepted with a visa linked to yours and can access the EAD (work permit).
