Night life
in San Francisco

In San Francisco there are different areas where you can enjoy nightlife despite the fact that most of the premises close before 2.30 am.
We tell you what are the best places in the city to have a few drinks:

South of Market (SoMa)

In South of Market (SoMa) you can find the most popular places in the city and it is one of the busiest areas at night. Almost all nightclubs and nightlife are located in this neighborhood, although depending on the time of year the popularity of the sites is changing.

North Beach and Chinatown

Along Columbus Avenue you can find a series of shops that stretch from Nort Beach to Chinatown, where you can enjoy interesting options for nightlife.

Union Square

The atmosphere on the street is very lively and where you will find the best Irish bars in the city. You can also visit the San Francisco Theater District, which offers shows that have been brought from Broadway, and allow you to enjoy an evening of exquisite etiquette.

Castro

There are the best places of environment in the whole city. There are plenty of places to enjoy group karaoke, drag shows and authentic venues where you can enjoy dancing without any limits.

Marina

This is the most recommended neighborhood for soletes (heterosexuals) who travel to San Francisco with the desire to find the love of their lives or someone to spend a night of passion with. It is an area of luxury atmosphere with high standing establishments and clientele of all ages. A good place to spend a night of drinks in the most elegant way you can think of in San Francisco.
