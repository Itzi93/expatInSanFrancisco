Don't you know what is the currency
in San Francisco?

The official currency of the United States is the US dollar or US dollar ($, USD, US $).

Bills and coins

You would find bills of 1, 2, 5, 10, 20, 50, 100 dollars and coins of 1 dollar and 50, 25, 10, 5 and 1 cent.

ATMs and banks

You will find ATMs in each part of San Francisco and also banks. If you don't have any ATM near you, you have the "cashback" option when you buy in a supermarket.

How can you pay?

The best way to save commissions is to always pay by credit card. But It will depend what bank you are and the comissions that they apply.

Where you can exchange money?

You can do it both in banks and in exchange offices, where normally they apply a huge quantity of comissions.
