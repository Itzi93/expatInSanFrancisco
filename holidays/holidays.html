National holidays
in San Francisco

  • New Years Day: January 1st.
  • Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. Day: Third Monday of January. Day in honor of the one who was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize and defended racial equality.
  • President's Day: Third Monday of February. Honors those who were presidents of the USA, with special remembrance to George Washington and Abraham Lincoln.
  • Memorial Day: Last Monday of May. Day in which members of the armed forces killed in combat are remembered.
  • Independence Day: July 4th. On July 4, 1776, thirteen colonies declared themselves free from English rule and formed the first states of the country.
  • Labor Day: First Monday of September.
  • Columbus Day: Second Monday of October. They celebrate the arrival of Christopher Columbus.
  • Veterans Day: November 11th. Day of celebration of the end of World War I and in honor of veterans.
  • Thanksgiving Day and the Day After (Black Friday): Last Thursday of November.
  • Christmas Day: December 25th.
