Discover all the neighborhoods
in San Francisco

In San Francisco exists a lot of different culture neighborhoods. Here you will find the most important:

Union Square

It is the most interesting and central area of San Francisco, where there is the highest concentration of activities for leisure and large number of people..

Restaurants

You will be able to find a great amount of restaurants and places where to eat dishes of heterogeneous origin: American, oriental, Italian, Mediterranean and even African food. The best restaurants according with Tripadvisor are: Union Square

Night Life

The atmosphere on the street is very lively and where you will find the best Irish bars in the city. You can also visit the San Francisco Theater District, which offers shows that have been brought from Broadway, and allow you to enjoy an evening of exquisite etiquette. The best clubs according with Yelp are: Union Square Night Life Union Square

Fisherman's Wharf

In 1853 the first port of San Francisco was built in this area; It quickly became an important marine center for the distribution of fresh fish until, in 1950, with the advent of modern technology, the bay area declined.

Restaurants

This old port is one of the ideal areas to eat seafood and fish. Therefore, if you are looking for a good paella or fish dish, do not miss out on this area. In it you will also find many street stalls where you can try cocktails of fish and crab and a variety of similar dishes. The best restaurants according with Tripadvisor are: Fisherman's Wharf

Night Life

Close to Fisherman's Wharf you will find Marina neighborhood. This is the most recommended neighborhood for soletes (heterosexuals) who travel to San Francisco with the desire to find the love of their lives or someone to spend a night of passion with. It is an area of luxury atmosphere with high standing establishments and clientele of all ages. A good place to spend a night of drinks in the most elegant way you can think of in San Francisco. The best clubs according with Yelp are: Fisherman's Wharf

Alamo Square

It is a colorful place that is characterized by its Victorian houses with its green and elegant gardens. This neighborhood is known by the name of "The Painted Ladies", refering to the houses, since they are painted with diverse and attractive colors.

Restaurants

They don't have a special food culture but you can find differents restaurants in this area. The best restaurants according with Tripadvisor are: Alamo Square

Night Life

If you live near Alamo Square you can find different places to go out clubbing. The best clubs according with Yelp are: Alamo Square

Yerba Buena Gardens

It's one of the most important parks in San Francisco because it's in the center of the city. In spring is a good place to enjoy music, your friends, etc.

Restaurants

The best restaurants according with Tripadvisor are: Yerba Buena Gardens

Night Life

If you are walking near Yerba Buena Gardens and you want to have some drinks, the best clubs are: Yerba Buena Gardens

Haight Ashbury

It is a neighborhood set in the 70s with a hippie and alternative atmosphere. In this neighborhood there are a variety of pubs where you can listen to live music.

Restaurants

In this neighborhood you will find differents dishes and so differents restaurants with really good food. The best restaurants according with Tripadvisor are: Haight Ashbury

Night Life

The best clubs near Haight Ashbury according with Yelp are: Haight Ashbury

Chinatown

in this neighborhood lives the largest Asian community outside of China in the world. You can feel out of San Francisco when you're there.

Restaurants

The Chinatown is the right place to savor oriental dishes, in many places very cheap. If you are a fan of Chinese food, these are the best restaurants where to take it: Chinatown

Night Life

Along Columbus Avenue you can find a series of shops that stretch from Nort Beach to Chinatown, where you can enjoy interesting options for nightlife. The best clubs are: Chinatown & North Beach

Japantown

This neighborhood is the oldest in the country. If you are interested in japanese culture, we recommend that you visit it.

Restaurants

From China to Japan, you won't miss any culture in this city. According with Tripadvisor the best japanese restaurants are: Japantown

Little Italy

Do you miss Europe? Keep calm! You can go to Little Italy and enjoy its restaurants or 'gelato'.

Restaurants

If you're looking for pasta and Italian food, your place is Little Italy. In addition, this corner of San Francisco will allow you to enjoy a colorful neighborhood with a lot of history. Tripadvisor recommend us these restaurants: Little Italy

Night Life

Near Little Italy there are a lot of clubs where you can spend your night with your friends. This neighborhood is so central and has a lot of night life. Here you will find the best clubs in this area: Little Italy

Castro

Since the 1960s, Castro has been known as the center of the gay community. You will find multicolored flags everywhere and a pleasant and open atmosphere. A magical corner where it could be said that there is no discrimination.

Restaurants

Castro is located near Market Street. Its location allows this neighborhood to have a lot of places where to taste differents foods. It's close to Mission, so there are a lot of hispanics restaurants. Here you have the best ones: Castro

Night Life

There are the best places of environment in the whole city. There are plenty of places to enjoy group karaoke, drag shows and authentic venues where you can enjoy dancing without any limits. The best places in this neighborhood are: Castro
