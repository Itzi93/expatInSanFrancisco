Best places with restaurants
in San Francisco

The gastronomic map of San Francisco is divided based on the type of food that tourists want to savor. In this way, depending on your favorite cuisine, you should go to one neighborhood or another.

Fisherman's Wharf

This old port is one of the ideal areas to eat seafood and fish. Therefore, if you are looking for a good paella or fish dish, do not miss out on this area. In it you will also find many street stalls where you can try cocktails of fish and crab and a variety of similar dishes.

Union Square

You will be able to find a great amount of restaurants and places where to eat dishes of heterogeneous origin: American, oriental, Italian, Mediterranean and even African food.

Chinatown

The Chinatown is the right place to savor oriental dishes, in many places very cheap.

Little Italy

If you're looking for pasta and Italian food, your place is Little Italy. In addition, this corner of San Francisco will allow you to enjoy a colorful neighborhood with a lot of history.

Meal schedule

Normally the American lunch is not the most important meal of the day, so it is usually eaten earlier and abundantly. The usual schedule of restaurants for lunch is from 11:30 to 14:30 and for dinner from 6:30 to 9:30.

Tips

Many restaurants include the tip in the account but, if not, it is usual to leave between 10% and 20% of the value of the account. Many of San Francisco's workers depend on tips.
