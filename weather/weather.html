How is the weather
in San Francisco?

Can you imagine having the 4 seasons of the year in the same day? In San Francisco the weather changes a lot, even in hours, depending of the depending on the ocean's breezes.
It is very common that the city is covered by a mountain of fog in any season of the year.

Winter in San Francisco

The winter in San Francisco is cold. January and February are the worst months because the temperature fluctuates between 5º and 14º. In this season when it rains most.

Spring in San Francisco

In spring, the climate is warm and temperatures fluctuates between 13º and 15º. In this season, the vegetation has many colors and the city looks prettier.

Summer in San Francisco

In summer, San Francisco is not hot, temperatures are not very high and there is always fog.

Fall in San Francisco

September and October are the best months in San Francisco. The temperatures are higher than the rest of the year and the sun is always up.
